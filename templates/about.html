Motivations

As fellow university students, we often struggle with challenging modules without a friendly Senpai we can turn to. However, we are positive that there are academically stronger students out there willing to help their peers out. Hence as part of the NUS Orbital programme, we created Teach Me Senpai, a platform for peer-to-peer teaching amongst university students.
